Communication Clinics

Communication clinics specialize in diagnosing and treating communication, language, speech, and swallowing disorders. They provide comprehensive services for children and adults experiencing various communication difficulties. The clinic staff includes expert speech therapists who use advanced treatment methods and innovative technologies to improve patients' communication abilities.

The table below presents the main services offered at the communication clinics of the health funds "Maccabi," "Meuhedet," and "Clalit," along with the benefits provided to insured members under different insurance plans. The services include:

  • Diagnosis of language and speech disorders: Comprehensive evaluation of language and speech abilities
  • Stuttering treatment: Advanced methods to improve speech fluency
  • Treatment of voice disorders: Rehabilitation and improvement of voice quality
  • Diagnosis and treatment of swallowing disorders: Evaluation and treatment of swallowing difficulties
  • Developmental delay treatment: Early intervention for children with language delays
  • Hearing rehabilitation: Support for users of hearing aids and cochlear implants
