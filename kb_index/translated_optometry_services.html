Optometry

Optometry is a field that focuses on eye health, vision, and the visual system. Optometrists are professionals qualified to perform vision tests, diagnose vision problems, and provide solutions such as glasses, contact lenses, and other optical aids.

The table below presents the main services in the field of optometry and the benefits available to members of the health funds "Maccabi," "Meuhedet," and "Clalit" under their various insurance plans. The services include:

  • Vision tests: Periodic tests to examine visual acuity and eye health
  • Eyeglasses: Fitting and purchasing frames and lenses
  • Contact lenses: Fitting and purchasing soft or hard contact lenses
  • Vision correction treatments: Procedures such as laser surgery for correcting nearsightedness
  • Special vision aids: Fitting devices for low vision and other accessories
  • Children's care: Dedicated optometry services for children and adolescents
