Dental Clinics

Dental clinics offer a wide range of oral health services, including routine check-ups, cleanings, fillings, root canal treatments, extractions, and cosmetic procedures. These clinics are staffed by licensed dentists and hygienists who use modern equipment to ensure quality care for patients of all ages.

The table below presents all the sub-services provided by dental clinics, along with the rates and benefits according to the insurance plans of the health funds "Maccabi," "Meuhedet," and "Clalit." The services include:

  • Dental Check-ups and Cleanings: Periodic examinations and professional cleanings
  • Fillings: Treatment for cavities and tooth restoration
  • Root Canal Treatments: Treatment for infections and inflammation in tooth roots
  • Crowns and Implants: Restoration of damaged or missing teeth
  • Orthodontics: Treatments to improve teeth alignment and bite
  • Cosmetic Treatments: Teeth whitening, veneers, and aesthetic smile improvements

Each service is detailed with the benefits available for the Gold, Silver, and Bronze plans in each health fund.
