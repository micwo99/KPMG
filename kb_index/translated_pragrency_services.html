Pregnancy

The pregnancy period is a significant time in every woman's life, accompanied by numerous physical and emotional changes. Health funds in Israel offer a variety of services and support for pregnant women to ensure a healthy and safe pregnancy. These services include medical monitoring, routine tests, educational courses, and preparation for childbirth.

The table below presents the main services offered to pregnant women by the health funds "Maccabi," "Meuhedet," and "Clalit," along with the benefits provided to insured members under various insurance plans. The services include:

  • Pregnancy monitoring: Regular visits to a gynecologist or midwife
  • Genetic screening tests: Tests to detect hereditary diseases
  • System scans: Comprehensive ultrasound examinations
  • Childbirth preparation course: Guidance and preparation for delivery
  • Nutritional counseling: Guidance for proper nutrition during pregnancy
  • Treatment for pregnancy complications: Monitoring and treatment of special conditions
