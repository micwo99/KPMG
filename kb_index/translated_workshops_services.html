### Health Workshops

Health workshops are an important part of the health promotion programs offered by health funds. They provide a wide range of educational and practical activities aimed at improving the health of members, imparting knowledge and skills for a healthy lifestyle, and assisting in coping with various health conditions.

The table below presents the main workshops offered by the health funds "Maccabi," "Meuhedet," and "Clalit," along with the benefits provided to members under different insurance plans. The workshops include:

  • Smoking Cessation: Workshops for quitting smoking and support during the process
  • Proper Nutrition: Guidance for healthy eating and balanced dietary habits
  • Physical Activity: Workshops to encourage and integrate physical activity into daily life
  • Stress Management: Techniques for coping with stress and anxiety
  • Diabetes: Guidance for managing the disease and maintaining a healthy lifestyle for diabetics
  • Pregnancy and Childbirth: Preparation for childbirth and baby care
